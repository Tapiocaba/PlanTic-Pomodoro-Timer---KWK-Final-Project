HISTORY

Plantic takes inspiration from ‘Pomodoro’ which is a time management method founded by Francesco Cirillo
that uses a timer to break down tasks into intervals. Our website divides your work time into 25 minute intervals
and gives you the option to pick between a short break and a long break!

HOW-TO

○ Choose a task to do.
○ Start the timer for 25 minutes and get to work!
○ Take a short, 5 minute break :)
○ Take a long break every 4 cycles!
